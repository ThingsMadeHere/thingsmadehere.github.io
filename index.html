Join us to build robots for the FIRST Robotics Competition.

Our Plan For the Year

Fall
  • Train and educate team members in safety, team communication, tool usage, and the robot design process, with hands-on experience and seminars.
  • Fundraise and friendraise.
  • Travel to and compete in an off-season competition and workshops (October 24 - 26).
  • Plan 2026 season travel.
Winter
  • Kickoff! We will have a potluck and learn what the game is for FRC 2026 on January 10th.
  • More intensive practice begins after that. Commitment level similar to a sports team.
  • Work as a team to determine our strategy for the competition.
  • Apply learned skills to iteratively design, build, and program the robot.
  • Practice competitions with other FRC teams.
Spring
  • Competition! Travel to at least two weekend-long district competitions (Likely March, dates TBD).
  • We will go to an additional district competition if we have funds, and the championship if we have the funds and manage to qualify.
  • After competition, teach robotics to local elementary and middle schools.
Summer
  • Off-season events!
  • Host free robotics events for nearby elementary and middle schools.
  • Low-pressure experiments with robot designs.
